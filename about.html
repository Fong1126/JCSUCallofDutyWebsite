Welcome to JCSU Call of Duty

We are a team of dedicated gamers from Johnson C. Smith University who compete in various tournaments and events throughout the year. Our team consists of talented players from a variety of games, including Overwatch, League of Legends, Dota 2, Valorant, and CS:GO.

We strive for excellence in everything we do, whether it's practicing for upcoming tournaments or helping out the community through charity events.

If you're interested in learning more about our team or want to join us, please feel free to contact us via email or social media.
